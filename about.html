Who I Am

I’m Douglass Brown, a multidisciplinary creative working across film, photography, sound, writing, and emerging media. My background began in visual production and expanded into leadership, web design, computer science, cybersecurity, UX, audio, and interactive systems.


I’m interested in the places where image, story, technology, and lived experience meet. I see media as more than content. It can be a record, a tool, a system, a ritual, and a way to make meaning.
